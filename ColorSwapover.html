>The rules of this is simple.

1)You just need to match the color of the ball and the bar! If the incoming ball is white, you need to change the color of bar to white and if the color of bar black you need to change the color of bar to black.

2)You can easily swap the color of the bar by pressing space or clicking anywhere on screen in Computer/Laptop and to change color of the bar in phone, you just need to tap any where in screen.

3)If white ball hit the bar when its color is black or black ball hit the bar when its color is white then game will be over.




>The scoring system of this game

1)For the first 10 balls you will get 1 point for every ball, then for then next 10 balls you will get 2 points each.

2)The points for each ball will keep growing like this until you reach 100th ball, after the 100th ball you will get 10 points for each ball.

3)After you reach 10 points for each ball,the point will stay constant for each ball. Means it wont grow anymore!




>About this game

The name of the game is color swap. It's a simple syntax and user-friendly game which everyone easily understands. Also you can make the graphics as you like from the settings. Even you can add some features from the settings (Like you can make the speed of ball show in game) and even you remove some features from the setting (Like you can make the score not show in game!)
